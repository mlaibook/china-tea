Matcha, caffeine, and why some people find it steadier than coffee

Created: 2026-03-16 · Updated: 2026-03-16

Some people experience matcha as less sharp and more stable than coffee. That perception may relate to caffeine amount, accompanying compounds such as theanine, drinking context, and personal sensitivity. It is better to treat this as a nuanced research topic than a simple ranking.
