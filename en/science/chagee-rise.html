The rise of Chagee: from regional brand to national tea chain

Created: 2026-03-16 · Updated: 2026-03-16

Chagee matters because it shows how Chinese tea can be repackaged into a national chain language: more visual, more branded, more standardized, and more easily replicated across cities. Its rise is not only about product taste. It is also about naming, store identity, “Oriental” brand packaging, operational repeatability, and the ability to turn tea into an urban lifestyle signal.
