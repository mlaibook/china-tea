Tea and metabolic health: what “healthy tea drinks” should actually mean

Created: 2026-03-16 · Updated: 2026-03-16

The key question is not whether tea has a magical ingredient. It is what the drink actually is: plain tea, lightly sweetened milk tea, or a sugar-heavy dessert-like beverage. Tea may be useful in metabolic-health discussions partly because it can replace worse beverage choices, not because every commercial tea drink is automatically healthy.
