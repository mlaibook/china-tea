About

China Tea Library is a bilingual editorial website about Chinese tea. It brings together tea leaves, teaware, tea history, modern tea drinks, and tea-related science in a structure designed for readers who want both clarity and depth.

The site is built around a simple idea: many people encounter Chinese tea through fragments—one famous tea name, one trending drink, one tea tool, one historical motif. This project tries to turn those fragments into a more readable whole, without flattening the richness of the subject.

Project link: https://github.com/mlaibook/china-tea
