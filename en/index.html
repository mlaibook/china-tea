Start here

If you want classic foundations, begin with Longjing, pu-erh, oolong, and gaiwan. If you want the site at its most current, move into Chagee, light milk tea, low-sugar tea drinks, and transparency debates around ingredients and tea bases. If you want older forms returning in the present, matcha, whisk culture, jianzhan, and other Song-related pieces make a good route.
