How to start browsing

If you want foundations, begin with Longjing, pu-erh, oolong, and gaiwan. If you want the site at its most current, move into Chagee, light milk tea, low-sugar tea drinks, ingredient transparency, L-theanine, and caffeine debates. If you want older forms returning in the present, matcha, whisk culture, jianzhan, and stove-boiled tea make a strong route.
