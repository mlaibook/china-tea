Why low-sugar tea drinks are booming: are consumers buying health, or the feeling of health?

Created: 2026-03-16 · Updated: 2026-03-16

Low-sugar tea drinks are rising because modern consumers want more than taste. They want drinks that feel easier to justify, easier to repeat, and less psychologically heavy than older sugary indulgence formats. In that sense, low-sugar tea drinks are not only a nutrition story. They are also a story about self-management, consumer identity, and the social language of “lighter burden.”
