## Topic-selection notes

This round compared multiple candidate directions against existing `zh/teaware` and `en/teaware` coverage:

1. 盖置 / lid rest
2. 茶则与茶荷 / tea scoop vs tea lotus
3. 银壶泡茶争议 / silver kettle for tea
4. 主人杯 / host cup
5. 提梁壶 / handled kettle or handled teapot

Reasons for selecting **盖置 / lid rest**:

- Strongest differentiation from existing published teaware pages.
- Fits current Chinese-internet discussion patterns around dry brewing, tabletop order, small but necessary support tools, and the question of where lids belong during active brewing.
- Supports a real long-form treatment of object logic, workflow, aesthetics, material choice, and common misunderstandings.
- Could be illustrated with directly relevant existing tea-table photos already tracked in the repo.
