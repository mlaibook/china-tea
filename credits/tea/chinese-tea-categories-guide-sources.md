## Scope
Beginner-facing bilingual overview of the main Chinese tea categories:
- green tea
- white tea
- yellow tea
- oolong tea
- black tea
- dark tea
- flower-scented and reprocessed tea as secondary branches

## Editorial goals
- Keep language plain and approachable
- Build a systematic first-time-reader map rather than an academic taxonomy
- Compare processing logic, flavor, brewing, region, stomach sensitivity, and caffeine feel in simple language
- Position the article as a featured / pinned hotspot entry for the tea section
